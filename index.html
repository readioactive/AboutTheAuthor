About the Author

Rea Kuske

Rea Kuske is a high school senior at Winnebago Lutheran Academy. She is involved with Student Council as Secretary/Treasurer, co-yearbook editor on the yearbook staff, and is involved in track. She has a younger brother who is a freshman and a sister who is a 7th grader. She also has two adorable cats named Smokey and Bandit. She is currently a cook at Bublitz's Restaurant and Alehouse in Lomira, Wisconsin. When looking into the future, she is considering Concordia University in Mequon, Viterbo University in La Crosse, and UW-Madison for Marketing and minoring in photography, or Martin Luther College in New Ulm, Minnesota to study secondary and elementary education and coaching.
